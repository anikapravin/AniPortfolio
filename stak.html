Design Brief

Create a logo for a Bangalore based Event management and curation company. The clients requested a logo that contained broad based imagery, that would not have any stereotypical symbolism. They wanted a look that would appeal to a larger variety of events. A terracotta colour and a minimal font was requested as well.

About the Brand

Stak Solutions is a Bangalore-based event management company that has been in the business for over two decades. They cater to all kinds of events, as well as sound and light installations. Previously the company did not have a visual logo, however in an attempt to rebrand and launch on various social media platforms - a new logo was designed. Their website and services are available at staksolutions.info.
